PAD CHARGE METER — INTEGRATED LED

The pad's existing cyan readout strip becomes a 3-segment charge LED. The same strip on the console face is split into three short cells; 1–3 light cyan for BIT's banked expedition charges. Nothing protrudes — it's the dock's own integrated level bar, so the pad stays exactly as slim as it is now. A lit strip = armed; tap the pad to send (spends one charge).
