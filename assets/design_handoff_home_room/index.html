Why this screen exists

Six product principles. The whole build serves these.

1Home is a place, not a stack of cards. Old widgets become things in the room.
2The face is the centerpiece, projected. BIT is the single brightest thing — you open Home and see you.
3BIT is the life and the explorer. The reacting creature you fuel with Core and send scouting.
4The room is wired to the lore. Every surface is the economy made visible.
5It pulls you back without pressure. Time-of-day window; the room rests, never sulks.
6It's built to grow. The wall is a modular grid — collection slots in without re-layout.
