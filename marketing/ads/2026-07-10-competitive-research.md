# Meta Ads — competitive research (2026-07-10)

Source: Meta Ad Library, pulled via the Meta Ads MCP connector on 2026-07-10 (ad account 794963308279471).
Method: keyword + competitor-page sweeps, US, plus one rendered competitor creative.

## TL;DR
- The **fitness-RPG niche is barely advertised** on Meta. One true direct competitor, one adjacent. The
  specific **pixel-art / retro-arcade** positioning has **zero** paid competition.
- The only direct competitor, **Fitness Empire Online**, is at the *same stage as you* (pre-launch:
  waitlist + closed beta) and is running ~14 Meta ads — which **validates the whole pre-launch playbook**
  (waitlist ads, beta ads, "choose your class," "earn XP," "level up").
- Their creative is **generic realistic high-fantasy art**. Ironbit's **pixel-arcade identity is a clean,
  ownable differentiator** in the feed.

## Direct competitor — Fitness Empire Online
- Page `1064628550057087`. ~**14 ads** (early 2026), Facebook + Instagram.
- **Hooks, by frequency:**
  - "Level up your fitness quest" ×8 ← their workhorse
  - "Choose Your Class" ×2  ← same as our Post 3
  - "Earn XP for all your workouts"
  - "Join the Waiting List"  ← they run explicit **waitlist** ads
  - "Closed Beta"  ← and **beta-recruitment** ads
  - a dynamic `{{product.name}}` catalog ad
- **Example creative** (`952539777723705`, ran 20 Mar–3 Apr 2026, now inactive) — see
  `evidence-fitness-empire-choose-your-class.jpg`:
  - Primary text: *"Earn XP from every workout!"*
  - Image: torchlit dungeon, muscular warriors mid-workout; overlay *"Level up your fitness and character
    in a game where exercise meets adventure."*
- **Read:** they prove waitlist + beta + class + XP hooks all work in this niche pre-launch. Their
  **weakness** is a generic epic-fantasy look (indistinguishable from any RPG, expensive to scale, not
  ownable) and static images (no motion).

## Adjacent / noise
- **AniFlex** — "Train Like an Anime Hero" (gamified fitness, anime angle). The nearest other player.
- "gamified workout app" (≈270 ads) is dominated by **non-fitness** (memoryOS brain-training, etc.).
- Broad "rpg / turn workouts into a game" terms return **millions** of ads — but that's the **mobile-gaming
  + micro-drama** ad ocean, not fitness. Not our competition.
- **"habit tracker rpg pixel" → 0 ads.** Nobody advertises a pixel-art RPG tracker.

## What it means for Ironbit's ads
1. **Lean hard into the pixel-arcade identity** — it's uncontested and scroll-stopping. It's the moat.
2. **Adopt the proven hooks, then out-execute:** "level up," "choose your class," "earn XP," and the
   pre-launch **"join the waitlist / beta."**
3. **Differentiator angle:** *"Not another epic-fantasy app — a pixel arcade you actually train in."* Plus
   **body-neutral** + **offline** angles the competitor doesn't use.
4. **Format edge:** they run statics; our **animated reel + RANK UP payoff** is a motion advantage.
